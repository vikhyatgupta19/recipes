Sev Tamatar Recipe

Rajasthani cuisine

Rajasthani Sev Tamatar Sabji is a delicious curry that comes in very handy when there are no vegetables at home and you still want to make a wholesome meal. This curry is made using juicy tomatoes which are saut√©ed with desi masalas and served with heaps of thick besan sev.

Ingredients

  • Bhujia Sev
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Spices (Red Chilli Powder, Turmeric Powder, Garam Masala Powder, Corriander Powder, Salt)
  • Cumin
  • Green Chillies
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin and crackle them.
  2. Then add ginger garlice paste & green chilies. Stir and saute for 10 to 12 seconds till the raw aroma of paste goes away.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes.
  4. Stir and then add spice powders - turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder and cumin powder.
  5. Stir and saute so that the tomatoes get cooked evenly.
  6. Add Water and let it boil.
  7. Add Bhujia Sev and let it cook for a minute and then switch off gas and serve
